About Me

Who is the face behind Maddie Foster Photography?

This is. This is the face. Or more like faces. These two people are why I do what I do!

My name is Madison Foster- but you can call me Maddie. I have a loving husband and a little baby boy who I love dearly! They are my whole world!

I am from Peoria, Arizona, but I live in Riverton, Utah now! However, I will always be an Arizona girl at heart! I love cats, (but dogs are fine too I guess) and plan on getting one at some point in my life. My boys like to keep me on my toes, and becoming a mom is one of the most rewarding things I have ever done in my whole life!

I have been doing photography and running my own photography free-lance business since I was 16! I absolutely LOVE taking pictures, it is one of the things that brings me most joy in life. I love capturing your moments, freezing you and how you are now in time to look back on for years, and generations, to come! I am a big proponent of taking pictures, or saving memories through them, because you will truly never regret the pictures you took down the road. You are constantly changing and forever adapting, so why not take the picture? Why not update the pictures on your wall to reflect the NEW you?

I hope to be in contact with you soon!
